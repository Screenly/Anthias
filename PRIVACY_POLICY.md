# Privacy Policy for Anthias

**Effective date:** 2026.06.04
**Last updated:** 2026.06.04

## Introduction

Anthias ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a free and open source application. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we have designed Anthias so that it does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Anthias does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personally identifiable information about you. Specifically:

- We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.
- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal identifiers.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies that identify you.
- We do not store your data on our servers, because the App does not transmit any data to us.

Any content, settings, or configuration you create within the App is stored locally on your own device and remains under your control.

## Permissions

If the App requests any device permissions, these are used solely to provide the App's core functionality on your device. The App does not use these permissions to collect or transmit personal data to us or to any third party.

## Third-Party Services

Anthias itself does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

Please note that when you download the App through an app store, the store owner may collect certain information in accordance with its own privacy policy, independently of the App. This is outside our control. We encourage you to review app store owner's own privacy policy.

If your particular deployment of Anthias connects to external content sources or services that you configure (for example, web pages or media URLs you choose to display), those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Data Security

Because Anthias does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data held by us that could be lost, stolen, or misused. Any information you store within the App stays on your own device, and securing that device is your responsibility.

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Since we do not collect or process any personal data, we hold no information about you to access, correct, or delete. You retain full control over any data stored locally on your device.

## International Users

The App can be used worldwide. Because no personal data is collected or transferred to us, there is no international transfer of your personal data resulting from your use of the App.

## Open Source

Anthias is open source software. Its source code is publicly available for inspection, which allows anyone to verify the data-handling practices described in this policy. You can review the source code here:

https://github.com/Screenly/Anthias

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
